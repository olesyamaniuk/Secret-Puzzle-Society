ABOUT THE GAME

Expose a secret society of puzzle-loving villains! Your best friend, Brooke, has stumbled upon the Secret Puzzle Society and is in over her head. Win a challenging match 3 levels, and search beautiful rooms full of secrets!.

FEATURES

  • MATCH 3 LEVELS

    Play addictive match 3 puzzles! Match colors to clear a variety of obstacles, and combine powerups for massive explosions. Enjoy hundreds of levels with more to come!

  • PUZZLE ROOMS

    Things are not what they seem! Explore interactive, 3D rooms that are full of secrets. Look behind paintings, examine curious objects, and crack into hidden spaces that hold the Puzzle Society’s most valued treasures!

  • COLORFUL VILLAINS

    The secretive Puzzle Society is full of eccentric criminals with dastardly plans! They work in the shadows and will go to great lengths to keep their identities a secret. Expose these villains by stealing their masks and forcing them to face justice!

  • FREE TO PLAY, NO ADS

    Secret Puzzle Society is free to play and never forces ads. This game does contain in-app purchases

HOW TO PLAY

  • Help Brooke catch the villains

    Get ready for an adventure full of tricks, traps, mazes and minigames. The only way to see behind the masks is to play!

  • Match 3 to master the adventure

    Play addictive match 3 puzzles to clear obstacles, combine power-ups for massive explosions, and search beautiful rooms full of secrets. Can you solve the puzzles?

  • Dive into centuries-old secrets

    Brooke’s investigation takes you from ancient hieroglyphs to chambers of mischievous power. But only if you can outsmart the villains and beat their clever traps. Do you have what it takes?

  • Make the story personal

    Collect the Secret Puzzle Society masks, decorate your investigation room and use your skills to dig deeper while the story unfolds. It's all in your hands – and on their heads. Maybe there's a mask with your name on it?
